2. Our Service

  1. OnlineRPL is an education facilitator connecting you with nationally recognised training via our partner Registered Training Organisations (“RTOs”).
  2. We are not an RTO. We do not issue qualifications. Instead, we:
    • Provide a Free Skills Assessment to determine your eligibility for RPL.
    • Guide you through our Online Application form.
    • Assist you in compiling and submitting your Evidence Portfolio.
    • Submit your complete application and evidence to the partner RTO for assessment.
    • Enable access to platform features: Enrolment, LLN Quiz, Referee Testimonial, Employment History, Evidence Portfolio, Statutory Declaration and Certification.
  3. To engage our Service you must:
    1. Complete the Online Application form in full; and
    2. Pay the specified Service Fee in accordance with Section 3.

4. Refund Policy

  1. You may be entitled to a full refund of the Service Fee under Australian Consumer Law if:
    • Your partner RTO refuses your application for enrolment;
    • Your partner RTO is unable to deliver the course for which you applied;
    • We cancel the Service before it begins.
  2. Partial refunds may be granted, at our discretion, if you withdraw your application in writing before submission to the RTO, less any non-refundable administration fee.
  3. All refund requests must be submitted in writing to refunds@onlinerpl.edu.au within 21 days of the event giving rise to the request.
  4. Approved refunds will be processed via your original payment method within 10 business days.

5. Timeframes

  1. You must submit all required documentation and evidence within six (6) weeks of our notification that your application is accepted.
  2. Requests for an extension must be emailed to compliance@onlinerpl.edu.au. Approval of extensions and any associated fees are at our discretion.
  3. Our partner RTO will assess your application within four (4) weeks of receiving your complete submission. We will notify you of the outcome by email.
  4. Upon successful assessment, your digital certificate will be issued by the RTO and made available via the Site within 14 days.

6. Regulatory Changes

  1. Occasionally, changes to training package requirements, licensing or RTO policies may occur. We will notify you of any such changes that affect your application.
  2. If new requirements necessitate additional evidence, fees or processes, we will provide you with written instructions. Failure to comply may delay or terminate your application.

7. Additional Costs

  1. Hard-copy certificates mailed to your nominated address incur postage and handling fees, as displayed on the Site.
  2. Rescheduling an in-person skills assessment with an RTO less than 48 hours before the appointment may incur a rebooking fee of $200.
  3. Any fees for retrieval of archived records or academic transcripts will be charged at our published rates.

8. Complaints & Appeals

  1. To appeal a decision by our partner RTO or by OnlineRPL, email complaints@onlinerpl.edu.au within 20 days of the decision.
  2. We will acknowledge receipt of your appeal within 5 business days and aim to resolve it within 30 days.
  3. If you remain dissatisfied, you may refer the matter to the relevant state or national training authority.
