RPL Roadmap for Students & Tradies

Navigate every stage confidently—from enrolment through to certification
1

Step 1: Navigating the Dashboard

  • Progress: See your status, upcoming tasks & notifications
  • Resources: Access guides, tips & support instantly
  • Personalized: Track your current stage & milestones
  • All-in-One: Everything you need in one place

Ensures you’re organized and informed.

Step 2: LLN Assessment

  • Instant Feedback: Gauge your readiness right away
  • Clear Guidance: Get targeted prep advice if needed
  • Seamless Process: Complete the assessment within RPL Online

Ensures you’re fully prepared to proceed.

3

Step 3: Enrolment

  • Simple Online Form: Streamlined online submission of your details
  • Instant Confirmation: Immediate acknowledgment to kick off your RPL application

Step 4: Identification

  • Secure Upload: Encrypted platform for your ID documents
  • Quick Verification: Fast confirmation of your identity
  • Seamless Progression: Continue your RPL journey without delay

Ensures a secure, uninterrupted process.

Step 5: Employment History

  • Simple Submission: Quickly enter or upload your work history
  • Showcase Skills: Highlight relevant experience and roles
  • Comprehensive Profile: Captures your full background and experience

Ensures your prior learning is accurately captured.

Step 6: Referee Testimonial

  • Automated Requests: Requests sent to your referees automatically
  • Seamless Collection: Referees submit via email; testimonials auto-uploaded
  • Integrated Workflow: External validations flow directly into your portfolio\

Ensures effortless collection of valuable testimonials to support your RPL application.

Step 7: Evidence Portfolio

  • Easy Upload: Submit documents, photos & videos in one place
  • Guided Selection: Use examples & guidelines to pick relevant evidence
  • Auto Alignment: Each file maps directly to course requirements

Showcases your real-world skills and advances you toward your qualification.

Step 8: Statutory Declaration

  • Confirm Authenticity: Verify that all evidence is genuine
  • Maintain Integrity: Uphold the credibility of your application
  • Secure Upload: Fast, encrypted submission process

Ensures trust and accuracy in your RPL application.

Step 9: Certification

  • Online Issuance: Official certificate generated once all steps are complete
  • Digital Delivery: Download your certificate instantly
  • Formal Recognition: Officially validates your skills and success

Marks the culmination of your RPL journey.
