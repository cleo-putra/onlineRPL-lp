OnlineRPL is a 100% online Recognition of Prior Learning platform that connects students and tradies with qualified assessors, streamlining your journey from enrolment through to instant digital certification.

Whether you’re an apprentice electrician, a hospitality student, a carpenter, a mechanic or any vocational learner, OnlineRPL fast‑tracks your skills recognition based on the experience you already have.

We partner with leading RTOs to offer nationally recognised qualifications in Carpentry, Automotive Mechanical, Patisserie—and more coming soon.

Most candidates complete their entire RPL process in days rather than weeks, thanks to instant online enrolment, built‑in compliance checks, and fast digital submissions.

On average, our learners have saved over $8,800 in course fees and cut 95% of the usual paperwork and wait times compared to traditional RPL methods.

By streamlining the entire RPL journey online, OnlineRPL removes tedious paperwork and postal delays so you can enrol and complete assessments in days, not weeks. Our pay‑as‑you‑progress pricing lets you only pay for the stages you need—no brokers or surprise fees—while intuitive on‑screen prompts, auto‑tracking and simple drag‑and‑drop uploads make submitting your evidence a breeze. With built‑in compliance checks and instant digital certification, we’ve turned a process that’s traditionally lengthy, costly and confusing into one that’s faster, cheaper and easier.
