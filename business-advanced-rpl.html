Business (Advanced) RPL

About The Qualification

The Business (Advanced) RPL program is suited to individuals who possess significant theoretical business skills and knowledge and wish to consolidate and build pathways to further educational or employment opportunities.

Scope of work

This program reflects the role of individuals with significant experience in a senior administrative role who are seeking to develop expertise across a wider range of business functions.

Experience Requirements

At least 5 years relevant industry experience in a wide range of business-related work where the majority of the experience was obtained within 10 years of the date on which the application is made.

Eligibility

100 Pts ID (JP Certified), Resume, Referee Statement, Qualification/Transcripts, Work Sample

Regulatory Licensing Requirements

No licensing, legislative or certification requirements apply to this qualification at the time of publication.

Individuals who possess sound theoretical business skills and knowledge and are looking for new opportunities or to advance their career can consider:

  • BSB40215 - Certificate IV in Business
  • BSB50120 - Diploma of Business
  • BSB60215 - Advanced Diploma of Business

Business (Advanced) RPL is a Recognition of Prior Learning program designed for experienced professionals looking to obtain formal qualifications in business management, strategy, and leadership roles. It is suitable for those with a background in supervising, managing teams, or leading projects across various industries.

Candidates must generally demonstrate a minimum of 3 years of experience in advanced business roles involving supervision, coordination, or project management. Evidence of this experience should be within the past 10 years.
