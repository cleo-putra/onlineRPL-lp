If you’ve worked in a related trade or industry for at least 1–2 years, either in Australia or overseas, you may be eligible. This includes tradies, care workers, admin professionals, supervisors, or anyone with relevant on-the-job experience.

You’ll need to show proof of your experience such as work photos/videos, resumes, reference letters, job contracts, invoices, or any prior qualifications and licences. We help guide you through this process step-by-step.
